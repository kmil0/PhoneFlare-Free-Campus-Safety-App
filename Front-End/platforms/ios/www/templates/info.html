Add contacts you want to notify during an emergency


Each one will recieve a text when you're in danger

This text will notify them of your location and status

Your GPS coordinates update for your contacts every 15 seconds until you are safe




If you're on or near campus, we call to notify your school of your location and status

Swipe up on any screen to begin an Emergency


Or arm PhoneFlare with an accessory in the headphone port and remove it



Or miss a check-in time you've set in advance.

Once you confirm you're safe, everyone is notified



And GPS access for contacts and campus safety stops

Options allow you to record audio during emergencies

MultiColor disarm lets you trigger silent emergencies and verifies your identity

  • Choose a color that is then required for disarm
  • Discretely activate an emergency by choosing the wrong color
  • Disarm online later from another device

    •

    If bluetooth is enabled, your phone will scan and log nearby smart devices during an emergency
